Homz Charcoal Gray 4 Leg Ironing Board

Enhance your ironing routine with the Homz Charcoal Gray 4 Leg Ironing Board, combining practicality and elegance for both home and professional use. The four sturdy steel legs provide a stable base, ensuring smooth ironing without any wobbling or instability. The perforated steel surface allows steam to distribute evenly, keeping your clothes cool and helping to remove stubborn wrinkles efficiently.

The 100% cotton cover is comfortable and long-lasting, designed to let your iron glide effortlessly across the fabric. Paired with a polyurethane foam pad, it offers a cushioned feel that protects your garments during use. With adjustable height settings from 32" to 40.5", this board is tailored to suit users of all heights, making it versatile for any setting.

When not in use, this ironing board folds compactly and locks securely, making it easy to store or transport. Its sleek charcoal gray cover and polished silver frame seamlessly blend with any laundry room decor, while the non-slip feet keep your floors safe and your board in place.
