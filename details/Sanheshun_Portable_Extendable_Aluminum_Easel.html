Sanheshun Portable Extendable Aluminum Easel

Elevate your art experience with this versatile and durable easel designed for artists, painters, and sketchers on the go. Whether you're working in your studio, creating in the wilderness, or showcasing your art at an event, this easel is built to adapt and perform.

Why Choose This Easel?

Featuring an adjustable height range of 57cm to 157cm, this easel lets you customize your workspace to suit your needs. The telescoping design makes it easy to extend or fold, and the included knobs and handle allow for precise control when setting the perfect angle.

Constructed from lightweight aluminum alloy, it balances durability with portability, ensuring strength without compromising on ease of transport. The foldable structure and tool-free assembly make it a breeze to store and carry, while the built-in triangular support keeps your canvas steady and secure, even under heavy use.

Perfect for Every Artist

With a removable tray that can hold up to 0.7 kg of art tools, you'll keep everything within reach and organized. The included storage bag ensures that your easel stays compact and safe when you're traveling, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor creative sessions.

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned professional, this easel offers the flexibility and stability you need to create with confidence. The non-slip rubber feet provide a secure grip on any surface, and the smooth hand crank ensures effortless adjustments whenever you need them.
